About us

Instrumental Dufour was found in 1939, and since then we have been providing all kind of services for magnetic compass. Not only, repairing and calibrating them but also, issuing worldwide authorized deviation curves and certificates.

We are the first company who introduced remote calibration for magnetic compass while navigating all around the world, making ships able to posses all documentation required by SOLAS whitout docking at ports or having a technicician on-board.

Our enterprice counts with NI-07 authorization from the argentinian Coast Guard Department (PNA), making us an authorized laboratory for repairing all kind of magnetic compasses and giving our certificates all necessary international validations to be presented to any Coast Guard Authority or organization.
